FADE IN:

EXT. DORIN - DAY

A great kingdom, bustling with life. The people are happy and content under the leadership of King Gelidor.

Suddenly, a dark force appears from the north. They attack without warning, and the people are forced to flee.

The kingdom is destroyed, and the people are left scattered.

Years later, a group of adventurers sets out to find the lost kingdom. They battle monsters, make new friends, and uncover long-forgotten secrets.

Finally, they reach the lost kingdom. They must face the enemy that destroyed it, and with courage and strength, they triumph.

Dorin is restored to its former glory.
