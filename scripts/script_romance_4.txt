INT. LINDA'S OFFICE - DAY

Linda is sitting at her desk, going through some papers. Her phone RINGS.

Linda: (answering) Hello?

MARK: (on the phone) Hey, Linda. It's Mark.

Linda: (smiling) Hey, Mark. What's up?

MARK: (on the phone) Not much. I was just thinking about you.

Linda: (laughs) Aww, that's sweet.

MARK: (on the phone) So, I was wondering if you wanted to go out to dinner tonight.

Linda: (hesitates) Um... sure. That sounds great.

MARK: (on the phone) Great. I'll pick you up at 7.

Linda: (smiling) Sounds good. I'll see you then.

MARK: (on the phone) See you soon.

Linda hangs up the phone and she can't help but smile.

CUT TO:

INT. RESTAURANT - NIGHT

Linda and Mark are sitting at a table, talking and laughing. They're having a great time.

MARK: (smiling) I'm really glad you gave me a chance.

Linda: (smiling) I'm glad I did too.

MARK: (serious) I promise I'll make it up to you.

Linda: (laughs) It's not like you did anything wrong.

MARK: (joking) I know. But I still feel like I need to make it up to you.

Linda: (smiling) I'll hold you to that.

They both laugh and continue to enjoy their evening.

CUT TO:

INT. LINDA'S APARTMENT - NIGHT

Linda is in her bedroom, getting ready for bed. She's smiling to herself, thinking about her date with Mark.

Suddenly, the phone RINGS. Linda picks it up.

Linda: (answering) Hello?

MARK: (on the phone) Hey, Linda. I just wanted to say goodnight.

Linda: (smiling) Aww, that's sweet. Goodnight, Mark.

MARK: (on the phone) I'll see you tomorrow.

Linda: (smiling) I can't wait.

They say their goodbyes and Linda hangs up the phone. She's still smiling.

CUT TO:

INT. LINDA'S OFFICE - DAY

Linda is sitting at her desk, working on some paperwork. The phone RINGS and she answers it.

Linda: (answering) Hello?

MARK: (on the phone) Hey, Linda. I was wondering if you wanted to go out to dinner tonight.

Linda: (hesitates) Um... I don't know, Mark. I'm really busy with work.

MARK: (on the phone) Please, Linda. I really want to see you.

Linda: (sighs) Okay. Fine. I'll go out with you.

MARK: (on the phone) Great. I'll pick you up at 7.

Linda: (sighing) See you then.

She hangs up the phone and she can't help but feel annoyed.

CUT TO:

INT. RESTAURANT - NIGHT

Linda and Mark are sitting at a table, not talking. Linda looks angry and Mark looks nervous.

Finally, Linda speaks.

Linda: (coldly) I thought I told you I was busy.

MARK: (nervously) I know. I'm sorry. I just really wanted to see you.

Linda: (sighs) I don't have time for this, Mark. I'm busy with work and I don't need any distractions.

MARK: (pleading) Please, Linda. Just give me another chance. I promise I'll make it up to you.

Linda: (hesitates) I don't know, Mark.

MARK: (pleading) Please, Linda. I really need you.

Linda: (sighs)Fine. I'll give you another chance. But you better not screw it up.

MARK: (relieved) I won't. I promise.
