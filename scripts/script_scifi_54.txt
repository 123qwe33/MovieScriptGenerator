FADE IN:

EXT. SPACE - DAY

A vast expanse of stars and planets. Among them is a small, blue planet.

CUT TO:

EXT. PLANET - DAY

A small planet on the outskirts of the galaxy. There is a colony of humans here, living in small huts.

CUT TO:

INT. HUT - DAY

Karen is a scientist who is working on a top secret project. She is in her lab, working on a new form of energy.

KAREN

(to herself)

This is it. The key to the future.

Suddenly, Karen discovers that the project is a fraud. The energy they are creating is not new, and it is not safe.

KAREN

(to herself)

This is impossible. I have to go public with this.

CUT TO:

INT. COLONY - DAY

Karen is brought before a group of scientists. She is accused of treason and put on trial.

KAREN

(to the scientists)

This project is a fraud. The energy you are creating is not new, and it is not safe.

The other scientists are forced to testify against her, and she is found guilty. She is sentenced to death.

CUT TO:

EXT. SPACE - DAY

Karen is sent to a prison planet. She is met by a group of other humans who are also sentenced to death.

CUT TO:

INT. PRISON PLANET - DAY

Karen and the other prisoners are given a chance to compete in a series of challenges. The winner will be given the chance to go free.

Karen decides to compete, and she quickly realizes that she is the only one who can win. With the help of her new friends, she makes it through the challenges and is freed.

CUT TO:

EXT. PLANET - DAY

Karen and her friends return to the colony and expose the project. The other scientists are arrested and the project is shut down.

Karen is hailed as a hero, and she realizes that even in the darkest of times, there is always hope.

THE END
