FADE IN:

EXT. DESERT - DAY

A vast and empty landscape. The only sound is the wind.

Suddenly, a helicopter appears on the horizon and approaches.

CUT TO:

INT. HELICOPTER - DAY

A group of people are inside, wearing hazmat suits. They are all silent and look scared.

CUT TO:

INT. QUARANTINE FACILITY - DAY

The helicopter lands and the people are led out by armed guards.

They are taken to a large room where they are given a briefing by a government official.

GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL

You are the lucky ones. You are the only ones who have survived the virus.

The rest of the world is gone.

You are here because we need you. We need you to find a cure for the virus.

You are the only ones who can save us.

The survivors are taken to their rooms. They are all shocked and scared.

They are watched by the government at all times. They are not allowed to leave.

CUT TO:

INT. LABORATORY - DAY

The survivors are working tirelessly to find a cure. They are making progress, but they know time is running out.

CUT TO:

INT. QUARANTINE FACILITY - NIGHT

The survivors are gathered in the common area. They are all tired and scared.

Suddenly, the power goes out. The room is plunged into darkness.

The survivors are panicked. They don't know what to do.

CUT TO:

INT. LABORATORY - NIGHT

The power outage has set back the survivors' progress. They are working by candlelight, but they are still making progress.

CUT TO:

INT. QUARANTINE FACILITY - DAY

The survivors are getting closer to a cure. They are working around the clock.

But the virus is spreading. More and more people are getting sick.

CUT TO:

INT. LABORATORY - DAY

The survivors have found a cure. They are celebrating.

But the virus is still spreading. There are more sick people.

CUT TO:

INT. QUARANTINE FACILITY - DAY

The situation is getting desperate. The survivors are doing everything they can, but the virus is winning.

CUT TO:

EXT. DESERT - DAY

The survivors are gathered outside the quarantine facility. They are waiting for a helicopter to take them to safety.

But the virus is spreading. People are getting sick.

The helicopter arrives and the survivors get on.

The helicopter takes off, leaving the sick people behind.

CUT TO:

INT. HELICOPTER - DAY

The survivors are relieved. They are safe.

But the virus is still spreading. There is no cure.

The survivors are left to wonder what will happen to the world.
