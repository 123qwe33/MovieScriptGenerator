INT. GRIMDARK FANTASY LAND - DAY Alyssa is born into a world of darkness and violence. She grows up witnessing all the cruelty and injustice in her land, but she refuses to give up hope. As she gets older, Alyssa decides that she wants to make a difference in her world. She becomes a warrior and fights against the tyrannical rulers, hoping to bring justice and peace to her land. Sadly, Alyssa's efforts are in vain and she is eventually killed in battle. However, her spirit lived on and inspired others to continue fighting for what was right. Even in death, Alyssa was able to make a difference in her dark and cruel world.
