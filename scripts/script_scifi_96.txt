FADE IN:

EXT. SPACE - DAY

A group of outlaws are fleeing from the authorities in their ship. They are being pursued by a number of other ships.

The outlaws end up stealing a ship and fleeing to a nearby planet. There, they find a lost civilization that is in danger of being destroyed by a hostile alien race. The outlaws band together to help the people of the planet and save them from destruction.

The film follows the outlaws as they try to save the people of the planet and stop the alien race from destroying the planet.
